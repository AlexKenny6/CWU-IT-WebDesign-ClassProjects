Home CMS Differences Why Use It

When it comes down to the money, Wordpress is the better way to move forward with your company, Pacwest Software Development LLC. There are many reasons why you should choose Wordpress over Squarespace and we will give you some reasons below.

  • Wordpress is a very easy way to add, edit, and maintain pages.
  • You can edit pages without touching any code.
  • There are plugins that can automate your business like an ecommerce plugin.
  • There are tons of themes you can choose from to create a beautiful website.
  • The Search Engine Optimization is easy to start and manage, this will allow your website to appear on Google searches.
  • The platform is open sourced so it is always getting better and free from restrictions that other platforms might have.
  • The company itself, Wordpress is over 10 years old and they are very trustworthy.
