We included a chart that has a bunch of numbers on it, but what does it mean?

A bit table is a two number system based on 1's and 0's.

Here is an example of a bit table in action. In order to create numbers from the chart, 1's and 0's must be used. In the example, a 1 from the two column, eight column, and thirty-two column are being activated. The computer will add those three imputs together and the end number is forty-two.
