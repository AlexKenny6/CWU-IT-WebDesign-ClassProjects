What Are Binary Numbers?

Home Bit Table Truth Table Bits Explained

Binary numbers are a very interesting creation that allow computers to turn on and programs to be created. It is based on a two number system of 1's and 0's. These two numbers are the beginning of creating computer programs.

For example, in the early years of computers, binary numbers were used to display text or images on the screen. However, everything was in black and white but it was a breakthorugh nonetheless. When a computer used the binary numbers, 0 represented off and 1 represented on. You can imagine that if text was being displayed, the actual lines in the letter were 1's and the black space between the letters were 0's.
