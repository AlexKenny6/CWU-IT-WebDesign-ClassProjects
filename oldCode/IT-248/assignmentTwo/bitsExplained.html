An easy way to understand what a byte is in English


A binary digit (1b) = 1 Bit

8 Bits = 1 Byte = 1One Letter in a word

1024 Bytes = 1KB (Kilobyte) = One basic page in a website

1024 KB = 1MB (Megabyte) = One basic website

1024 MB = 1 GB (Gigabyte) = 1/256th of an iPhone storage

1024 GB = 1 TB (Terabyte) = 15 games on xbox

With the information on the left, you will be able to understand what people are talking about when they say "byte" or "binary".
