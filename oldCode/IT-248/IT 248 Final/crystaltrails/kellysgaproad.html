Kelly's Gap Road

Next to the Green Valley Express Lift

Photo creds: On The Snow

Why We Love It


Kelly’s Gap Road is a fun and exciting trail that we like to go on. On the trail map it is a blue however it could turn into a green on some parts of it. It is a long trail that has a lot of curves and straights to it including a lot of potential to go into the trees and make your own trail. Our favorite part is near the lower end of the trail, it is a twisty section that allows you to go slow and then fast and maybe get some air on the edges. The bottom of the trail takes you back to the lodges where you can hop on the Mt Rainier Gondola or Chinook Express for more action.

Why You'll Love It


Kelly’s Gap Road is a blue run that is not difficult but not easy. Right in the middle because the terrain changes and you have to know how to slow down. You can be a beginner skier or snowboarder and test your skills on this run however we do not recommend it. Aside from that, the trail is a fun experience and one you’ll not forget because you’ll be on trail that is in the trees. It has several curves and some steep downhill to it but once you’re in the trees it is pretty flat. There is the opportunity to get some air on small jumps on the sides and at the bottom the lodges will be awaiting you for a break.
