Basketball Shoes are Fashion: A Project Scope

Subject: To market basketball shoes to both people who want to play basketball and those who want to wear them as fashion.

Age Group 1: Basketball Players 10-18

Age Group 2: Non-basketball players: 15-25

Pages will be different: different background images and content will be focused towards that target group.
CSS styling will be different, basketball will be about playing the game vs non-basketball will be about the next hotest shoe trend.
Color scheme will be different between the two. A focus on blue will be used for basketball and black/red will be the non-basketball store.
